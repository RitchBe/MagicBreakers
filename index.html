WHO WE ARE

" I saw professional people doing professional thing at the right place and the right moment. Everybody was happy " Spectator

Magic breakers assembles breakdance, acrobatics and comedy in various representations going from the street performance to more traditional showcase. The team is composed of Acro, the kangooroo and acrobat of the crew, Tibs, the seductive and entertaining host and Havok, the young model and nomad breakdancer. These three artists have years of experience as entertainers and have already been travelling, pleased to their art, all around the globe (London, Sydney, LA, Lisbon,…). Their spectacular movements which defy the laws of physics, the good vibe that is brought to the audience through their positive energy and musicality and the inspirational message addressed to the kids are the main strengths of these modern entertainers.
