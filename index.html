WHO WE ARE

" I saw professional people doing professional thing at the right place and the right moment. Everybody was happy " Spectator

Magic breakers assembles breakdance, acrobatics and comedy in various representations going from the street performance to corporate meeting or more traditional showcase for venues or private parties. The first member of the team is Acro, the kangooroo and acrobat of the crew, who has previously worked for circque du soleil in a representation in Las Vegas. Following this, Tibs, the winner of Australian street performance 2017, is the seductive, funny and entertaining host of the group. Finally, Havok, the headspine master, is the young model with a magic charisma that captivates the audience’s attention. These three artists have years of experience as entertainers and have already been travelling all around the globe by means of their art (London, Sydney, LA, Lisbon ...). Their spectacular movements, which defy the laws of physics, the good vibe that is brought to the audience through their positive energy and musicality, and the inspirational message addressed to the kids, are the main strengths of these modern entertainers.
