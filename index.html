WHO WE ARE

" I saw professional people doing professional things at the right place and the right moment. Everybody was happy !" Peter, Spectator

Magic Breakers brings breakdance, acrobatics and comedy together in various representations going from street performances to entertainment for corporate meetings or more traditional showcase for venues or private parties. The team is composed of three dancers, each with their own unique skills. These three artists have years of experience as entertainers and have already been travelling all around the globe by means of their art (London, Sydney, Los-Angeles, New-York, Lisbon, ...). As a multicultural group, Magic Breakers strives to promote unity and love during the entire performance. Their acrobatic movements defying the laws of physics combined with their positive energy are some of the main reasons why these modern entertainers are able to build large and engaged audiences anywhere in the world. Professional, surprising and dynamic, Magic Breakers systematically stands out amongst other acts.

THE TEAM

Acro

Jeremie Houet, alias Acro, is the jumper of the crew. Born in 1991 in Belgium, Jeremie is a professional acrobat who previously worked for "Cirque du Soleil". At the age of 18, he began his career as a street artist while studying in Los Angeles. He is one of the few street entertainers in the world who can flip over 9 persons on the concrete.

Tibs

Thibault Mundele also known as Tibs, is the host that entertains the crowd with his words and humour. He was born in 1990 to a Belgian mother and a Congolese father. He started dancing from a very young age and specialised in breath taking one hand balance. Thibault has travelled and performed in 24 different countries for various type of representation. In 2017, he was invited to give a speech for the prestigious TED Talk and in the same year, he won the title of “Winner of Best Street Performers” in Australia.

Ritchy

Richard Houet, Jeremie's brother, also known as Ritchy, is the youngest member of the team. Born in 1995 and raised in a family with 3 older brothers already addicted to acrobatic disciplines, he started practising gymnastic and breaking moves really young. Since then, he has won several breakdancing competition in various countries.



T-co

Bboy T-co (The Chosen One) born as Jerry Maxeus to Haitian parents. T-co started dancing in the mid 90’s and over the past decade he has become one of the worlds top-tear street performers. Diverse with a mix brand of witty comedy and charisma.
